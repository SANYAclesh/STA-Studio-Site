// QUESTIONS

FAQ

What is STA Studio?
+
STA Studio is a School Team Art Studio — a creative team of students passionate about game development, art, and interactive storytelling. We create original games and products driven by creativity and collaboration.
When will TLOL be released?
+
The Limit Of Lies (TLOL) is currently in active development. We're working hard to deliver a polished experience. Follow us and sign up for notifications to be the first to know the release date!
Is TSR free to play?
+
The Street Racer (TSR) is planned to launch as a free-to-play title. Our focus is on getting the game into as many hands as possible so everyone can experience the unique physics-based racing gameplay.
What is Crystal Realms?
+
Crystal Realms is our most ambitious project yet. Details are still under wraps — we'll reveal more information as development progresses. Stay tuned and sign up for notifications!
How can I contact or join the team?
+
We're always open to talented and passionate individuals! If you'd like to collaborate or join STA Studio, reach out through our social channels. We look forward to hearing from you!
Where do you ship store items?
+
Currently we ship to select countries in Europe and North America. Worldwide shipping is coming soon! All orders are processed within 3–5 business days and you'll receive a tracking number once your item ships.
